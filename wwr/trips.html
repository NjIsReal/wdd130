Family Fun Day Trip

This trip is perfect for families and first-time rafters. Enjoy a gentle scenic float down the river with an experienced guide, followed by a delicious riverside lunch. It's a great way to introduce your loved ones to the beauty of the outdoors.

Wilderness Explorer Overnight

For those seeking a true adventure, this overnight trip is for you. We'll tackle some challenging rapids, set up camp on a remote beach, and enjoy a campfire meal under the stars. All gear and meals are provided.
